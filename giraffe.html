GIRAFFE

The South African giraffe is found in northern South Africa, southern Botswana, southern Zimbabwe, and southwestern Mozambique.

It has dark, somewhat rounded patches "with some fine projections" on a tawny background colour. The spots extend down the legs and get smaller as they do.

The median lump of males is less developed. 52 A maximum of 31,500 are estimated to remain in the wild and around 45 are kept in zoos.

Height: Males:- 4,6 - 6,1m (Adult), Females:- 4 - 4,9m (Adult), also Mass: Females:- 550 - 1200kg (Adult), Males:- 800 - 930kg (Adult)
